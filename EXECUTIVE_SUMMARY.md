## 🎓 Key Insights

### Finding #1: Past Behavior is Everything
Customers who accepted previous campaigns are **10x more likely** to respond.
- 0 past acceptances → 8% response
- 4 past acceptances → 91% response

**Action:** Build "engaged customer" fast track, stop targeting never-responders.

### Finding #2: Wine is the Golden Product
Champions and Loyal Customers spend **45-55% of budget on wine**.
- $629 average wine spend for Champions
- Over-index by 300% vs overall average

**Action:** Lead with premium wine in all campaigns, create wine subscription.

### Finding #3: Channel Matters
- **Empty Nesters:** Love catalogs (30% of purchases)
- **Young Families:** Store-dominant (65%)
- **Digital Natives:** Web-first (37%)

**Action:** Match channel to segment, invest in premium catalogs for high-value customers.

### Finding #4: Untapped Categories
Fruits, Fish, Sweets represent only **7-9% of spend** across all segments.

**Action:** Bundling strategy (Wine + Cheese + Fruit) can increase basket size 20-30%.

### Finding #5: Don't Discount Champions
High-value customers buy on **quality, not price**.
- Champions: 8% of purchases are deals
- Low-value: 28% of purchases are deals

**Action:** Reserve discounts for reactivation only, never train Champions to wait for sales.

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Model Drift
**Issue:** Customer behavior changes over time  
**Mitigation:** Refresh RFM scores monthly, retrain model quarterly

### Risk 2: Over-Focusing on Past Responders
**Issue:** Missing new high-value customers  
**Mitigation:** Reserve 10% of campaign for exploration/testing

### Risk 3: Segment Fatigue
**Issue:** Over-contacting Champions  
**Mitigation:** Frequency caps (max 1 campaign per month per customer)

### Risk 4: Execution Complexity
**Issue:** Segment-specific creative is more work  
**Mitigation:** Start with 3 templates, expand gradually
